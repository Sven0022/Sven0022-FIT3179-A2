About Malaysia

Malaysia, a Southeast Asian country, is known for its rich biodiversity, tropical climate, and vibrant culture. The country is committed to sustainable development goals, with initiatives in reducing air and water pollution, improving sanitation, and increasing access to clean energy. In recent years, Malaysia has taken steps to monitor river basin pollution and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
